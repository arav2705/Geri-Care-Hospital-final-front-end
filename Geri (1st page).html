Mini-Cog Test
Answer these queries to test cognitive functionality.

The Mini-Cog is a brief cognitive test that involves an assessment of an older person's ability to recall three words and draw a clock. It is one such neuropsychological test that has been effective in detecting patients with dementia.

My Nutrition Score!

The ‘My Nutrition Score’ report which is generated on the basis of the information provided by the individual is based on research and scientific evidence in the field of nutrition and dietetics. A healthcare professional can use this report to help an individual achieve optimal nutritional status.
